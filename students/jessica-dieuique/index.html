Best Travel Experience

To mark the start of something new, I decided to go back to my roots and discover more about the country where my parents were born. This past December, I left for Vietnam to spend 5.5 weeks eating and exploring...and eating some more. (Vietnamese food is INCREDIBLE, by the way!).

It was a trip with a little bit of everything - a wedding, lots of family (and some drama), adventure, delicious food, ocean & beaches, and so much history. I met many relatives for the first time, and learned more about my parents, grandparents and Vietnam to take home with me. I left feeling grateful to have been born and raised in Canada, but glad to be able to (re)connect with my heritage. My life goals are to improve my Vietnamese and visit more often! ♥

Side note: The cover photo is of Nha Trang, the city where my grandparents and many uncles, aunts and cousins still live.
