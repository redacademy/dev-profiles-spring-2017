Favourite Travel Experience

In 2009, I went to Australia and did a working holiday. I lived and worked at a hostel in Cairns where I got a part-time job driving the hostel's shuttle bus. I learned to drive a right-hand-drive vehicle with a manual transmission, which was challenging but rewarding. Everyday I would make multiple trips to downtown and back picking up or dropping off guests of the hostel. It was a fun job for a few months and allowed me to experience real life in a small Australian town.
