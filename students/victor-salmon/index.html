Personal Flair

Me: A Collection of Communities

I've always been fascinated by culture and how it impacts found family and pair bonding. This has led me to volunteer, explore, and sometimes teach in various different communities. I'm originally from Ladner and love hand-feeding birds at Reifel Bird Sanctuary.

I'm uniquely qualified for most Peoples Teams being familiar with many alternative lifestyles. I also have lots of experience with training. Having been in several network marketing companies, I've constantly been doing personal development and team training. I've also facilitated consent workshops with the Vancouver Consent Crew and taught consensual power dynamics for MetroVancouver Kink. I've also volunteered as a Big Brothers mentor for the last seven years or so and continue to do so. I identify as queer and as a person of colour, giving me lived experience and a unique perspective.
